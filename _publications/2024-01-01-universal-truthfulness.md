This paper presents an analysis of the universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models. We identify a geometric structure that captures the truthfulness behavior of LLMs and propose methods to leverage this structure for improved model reliability.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
