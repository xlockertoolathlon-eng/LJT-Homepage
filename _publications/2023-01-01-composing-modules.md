This paper explores methods for composing parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations. We demonstrate how to combine different parameter-efficient fine-tuning modules to achieve better performance with minimal additional parameters.

**Authors:** Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He
