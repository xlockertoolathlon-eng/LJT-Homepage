This paper proposes in-context sharpness as a method for detecting and mitigating hallucinations in language models. From an inner representation perspective, we show how sharpness in in-context examples can serve as alerts for potential hallucinations.

**Authors:** Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He
