This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in vision-language models (VLMs) specifically for chart understanding tasks. We identify key limitations in current VLMs and propose solutions to improve their chart comprehension capabilities.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He
