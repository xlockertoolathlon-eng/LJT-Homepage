Welcome to my academic homepage! I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://www.cs.hku.hk/~jhe/) at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

## About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at HKUST, where I work under the supervision of Professor Junxian He. My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with specific interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Understanding and improving how large language models reason about complex tasks
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**: Investigating and mitigating hallucinations in multimodal models
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Developing methods to make LLMs more truthful and interpretable
